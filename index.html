About Fancy Chinese Clinic

To help you stay healthy, feel your best and age well by delivering effective treatment which combines traditional Chinese medicine and modern technology. From acupuncture to herbal medicine to advanced gene technology, lots of symptoms and conditions can be treated, improved and prevented, such as, pain relief (all kinds), stress and insomnia, overweight, skin care, respiratory illness, and neurological problems.

  • More than 20 years of clinical experience (both China and Canada)
  • Registered Traditional Chinese Medicine Practitioner (R.TCMP) & Acupuncturist (R.Ac)
  • Registered Ontario Osteopathic & Alternative Medicine Manual Practitioner
  • Registered Toronto Municipal Licensing & Standards Holistic Practitioner
  • Successfully treated stroke, facial paralysis, herpes zoster, painful obstruction, allergies, weight loss, and hair growth
  • Received the Global Outstanding Chinese Award in 2008 from Chinese21.com
  • Speaks Korean, English, and Chinese
  • Personal Motto: Prevention Today, Healthy Aging Tomorrow.
